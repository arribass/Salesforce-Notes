# What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It encompasses a wide range of technologies and techniques that enable machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding. AI can be categorized into two main types: narrow AI, which is designed to perform specific tasks, and general AI, which has the ability to perform any intellectual task that a human can do. AI is used in various applications, including natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and machine learning, and it has the potential to transform industries and improve efficiency in many areas of life.
